Antioxidant and Anti-inflammatory Effects

A randomized controlled trial published in Scientific Reports (2024) demonstrated that Cordyceps militaris exhibits potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects, which help combat oxidative stress and inflammation-related diseases.

What was the study about?

Scientists wanted to test if taking Cordyceps militaris, a type of medicinal mushroom, could help improve health by reducing two major problems:

  • Oxidative stress – when harmful molecules (free radicals) damage our cells.
  • Inflammation – the body’s response to injury or illness, which, if uncontrolled, can lead to diseases like diabetes, heart problems, and arthritis.

What did they do?

  • They conducted a randomized controlled trial (a reliable scientific method).
  • Two groups of people participated:
    • One group took Cordyceps militaris supplements daily.
    • The other group took a placebo (a harmless pill with no active ingredients).
  • The trial lasted for several weeks, and researchers measured markers of oxidative stress and inflammation in their blood.

What did they find?

  • The group that took Cordyceps militaris had lower levels of oxidative stress. This means fewer harmful molecules were damaging their cells.
  • They also had reduced inflammation, which could help lower the risk of chronic diseases.
  • Overall, the participants reported feeling more energetic and healthier.

What does this mean for you?

Taking Cordyceps militaris as a supplement might be a natural way to protect your cells, reduce harmful inflammation, and potentially improve your energy and overall health.

However, always consult a doctor before starting any new supplement!

Citation: Singh, R., et al. (2024). "Cordyceps militaris supplementation reduces oxidative stress and systemic inflammation in humans: A randomized controlled trial." Scientific Reports. DOI: 10.1038/s41598-024-58742-z.
